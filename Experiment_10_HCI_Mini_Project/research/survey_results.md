# Survey Results Summary

**Participants:** 12 university students

**Key Findings:**
- 7/12 use mobile apps or digital tools for planning
- 8/12 struggle with procrastination and forgetting deadlines
- Most requested features: reminders, progress tracking, calendar view
- Preferred views: 6/12 like Kanban boards, 4/12 prefer lists
- 9/12 use smartphones for planning
- Suggestions: Simpler UI, motivational features, better notifications

**Implications:**
- Students want easy-to-use, visually organized planners
- Mobile-first design is important
- Reminders and progress tracking are top priorities
